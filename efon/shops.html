Our showrooms are conveniently located in the town square of Tranquility, Naboo at -3427,5851 (approximately 2km NE of Theed) and southwest of Mos Entha, Tatooine at 630,2786. The Naboo showroom displays every type of droid available, so make a trip out to Efon's Droids to choose between a vicious doideka, a massive Binary Load Lifter, a shiny Protocol Droid, or any of the other in our wide variety of droid companions. We also stock droid batteries, customization kits, and other miscellany on the vendors in the shop. Both locations also feature fine starships and parts by Master Shipwright Esef.
