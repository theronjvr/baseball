Below are brief descriptions of the droid accessories and other products available through Efon's Droids. Please to refer to our pricing page for current prices on the accessories.

Starship Accessories
Flight Computer
A portable data storage device for non-Rebellion ships to hold flight command programs. There are six levels of flight computer data capacity: v1: 20, v2: 40, v3: 70, v4: 110, v5: 125, and v6: 150. The complexity of and mineral requirements for making these modules vary.
Starship Paint Kits
These kits are used to color the trim and body of a starship (NOTE: Imperial ships are not customizable).
Starship Texture Kits
These allow customization of ships with different pattern styles. See here for patterns available on different ship classes. (NOTE: Imperial ships are not customizable).
Unprogrammed Droid Command Modules
These are burnable data chips that are programmed to hold one droid program among the pilot's learned commands. After use, the "ready chip" is transferred to the droid or flight computer datapad.

Droid and Vehicle Accessories 
Droid Battery
Sold in crates, these provide power to the droid and are required by all models. Each battery has 3-4 charges, and is used by selecting "recharge" in the droid's radial menu.
Vehicle Customization Kit
These kits are used to color the trim and body of a vehicle. The application of these colors will fade after time, and need to be reapplied.

Droid Chassis/Colors
Efon's Droids offers custom-named droid decorations, which are not limited by the name filter, by special order (please send an in-game mail to Efon to reserve your decoration). New chassis (Droideka, Battle Droid, Mining Droid MK3) are available, but not yet shown below.


Color is a tint over black, not solid like other paints.

Available in Gold Only
